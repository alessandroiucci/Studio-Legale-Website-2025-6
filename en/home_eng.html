Who We Are

Carloni Law Firm was founded with the goal of providing personalized legal advice and assistance, grounded in experience, expertise, and dedication. For years, we have been guiding individuals, businesses, and professionals through complex legal matters, offering a pragmatic approach and concrete solutions, always fully respecting the rights and interests of our clients. Thanks to a team of qualified and continuously updated lawyers, we are able to handle issues in civil, commercial, corporate, real estate, and condominium law, as well as provide support in both judicial and extrajudicial disputes. We believe in a direct and transparent relationship with our clients: we carefully listen to every need to jointly develop the most effective strategy, emphasizing clarity in communication and promptness in our responses. Our mission is to become a reliable point of reference for those seeking a legal partner capable of offering protection, innovative solutions, and tangible results, always placing the individual and their needs at the center. We are based in Rome, at Via Filippo Corridoni 14, but we support clients throughout Italy thanks to modern technologies that allow us to provide continuous and qualified assistance. Carloni Law Firm — your legal security, every day.
