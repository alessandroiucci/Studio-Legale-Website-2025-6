Condomini

Contact Us

Our firm provides legal advice and assistance in the field of condominium law, supporting property managers, owners, and tenants in handling issues related to condominium living. We assist with the drafting and review of condominium regulations, recovery of unpaid condominium fees, challenges to shareholders’ meeting resolutions, disputes between co-owners, mediation, and out-of-court dispute resolution. We work to ensure the proper management of common areas and harmonious coexistence among residents, offering clear and effective solutions in full compliance with current regulations. Rely on us to protect your condominium rights and to resolve any conflicts promptly and professionally.
