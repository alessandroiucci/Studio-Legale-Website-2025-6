We provide specialized legal advice in corporate law, assisting entrepreneurs, companies, and startups at every stage of their business lifecycle. We handle company formation, drafting of articles of association and shareholders’ agreements, extraordinary transactions (mergers, acquisitions, demergers), transfer of shares or quotas, corporate governance, and relationships among shareholders. We offer assistance in managing relations with corporate bodies, resolving shareholder disputes, and addressing issues related to directors’ liability and regulatory compliance. Our objective is to safeguard your business with clear and effective solutions, providing ongoing legal support to help your company grow securely and confidently.
